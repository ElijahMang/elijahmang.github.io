Who I Work With

Clients who are building something worth protecting.

Homebuyers

New homeowners taking on their largest financial liability. Often the first real conversation about protection they have ever had. The right coverage at closing protects the mortgage, the family, and everything they are building toward.

Real Estate Investors

Portfolio builders accumulating equity across multiple properties. The conversation is not just about what happens at death. Business Owners

Owners with wealth tied up in the business and no efficient way to move it, protect it, or transfer it. The business keeps running if something happens, and the owner builds a permanent asset alongside it.
